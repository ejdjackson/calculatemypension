Your Current Situation

The starting point for your retirement calculations. If you have mutiple funds, please provide a combined value.

Tick these boxes for additional features as required.

Your Partner's Current Situation

Add your partner's details here. If they have mutiple funds, please provide a combined value.

Your Retirement Income Goals

An important part of retirement planning is estimating how much you will need to cover your expenses in retirement whilst also having sufficient income for entertainment and holidays. Specify your desired income here in today's money terms.

The Retirement Living Standards website offers a practical framework to help you estimate the income needed for your desired lifestyle in retirement.

When planning as a couple, the calculations assume that you both retire at the same time and both take the same percentage of tax-free cash.

Future Contributions

How much are you currently contributing to your funds each month? If your employer makes contributions to your pension, please include these.

Partner's Future Contributions

How much is your partner currently contributing to their funds each month? Include any employer contributions.

Defined Benefit Pension

If you have a guaranteed pension payable from a certain age please add details here. This may be a final salary or career average earnings pension from a current or previous employer.

Partner's Defined Benefit Pension

If your partner has a guaranteed pension payable from a certain age please add details here. The income from this will be brought into the calculations at the specfied age.
