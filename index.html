Welcome to Calculate My Pension

Are you asking yourself:
When can I afford to retire?
Am I saving enough into my pension?
How much can I withdraw from my pension fund?

Calculate My Pension is a powerful financial model designed to help you find the answers to these questions. A user-friendly interface allows you to enter the details of your current retirement provision and ongoing saving along with your goals for retirement. It also allows you to adjust the assumptions in the model. The results are instantaneous: the level of income you will be able to afford in retirement, given the inputs. The Dashboard then allows you to experiment with your inputs, to investigate scenarios and to answer the above three questions.

Calculate My Pension also aims to educate you about all aspects of the model and pensions in general so you can better understand the implications of the results. There is a Learn page that has all the information you need in one place. There is also a quiz. In fact, it is worth attempting the starter quiz before trying the calculator so you can identify any gaps in your pensions knowledge.

For premium members, Calculate My Pension provides additional functionality to tailor your cashflows and investigate scenarios. It also allows you to plan as a couple. If you are already retired, it will help to answer the key question of how much income you can afford to take allowing for the risk of running out of funds.

What You'll Learn About Your Pension

Sustainable Retirement Income
The maximum sustainable income you can withdraw without running out of funds will be calculated. This is done by simulating your retirement many times and adjusting your income up or down based on the funds remaining each time.
Fund Progression
The progression of your funds over time will be plotted in a chart, showing the accumulation of your funds up to retirement and then the drawdown phase as income is taken.
Retirement Income Breakdown
You will see a breakdown of your retirement income between the various sources in each retirement year, including your state pension, and allowing for the different sources to commence payment at different ages.
The Effect of Assumptions
You will be able to alter the assumptions for fund growth, inflation and charges. Then see the effect of these changes on your projected retirement income.
