What are the outputs?
Maximum Affordable Retirment Income
The calculator takes into account your pension and ISAs and calculates the maximum sustainable income you can afford to take at your chosen retirement date, allowing for your state pension and any defined benefit pension.
Retirment Income Breakdown
A bar chart showing the breakdown of your retirement income between the various sources in each retirement year, including your state pension, and allowing for the different sources to commence payment at different ages.
Fund Progression
A graph of the progression of your funds over time, accumulating with contributions and growth up to retirement and then being drawn down as income in retirement.
