Welcome to Calculate My Pension

Are you asking yourself:
When can I afford to retire?
Am I saving enough into my pension?
How much can I withdraw from my pension fund?

Calculate My Pension is designed to help you find the answers to these difficult questions. It offers personalised insights into your financial situation, enabling you to explore various retirement scenarios that are relevant or concerning to you.

If you are already retired and drawing your pension, Calculate My Pension can assist you in determining a sustainable ongoing income to support your lifestyle. For those planning ahead for retirement, it helps you identify the necessary contribution levels to achieve your desired level of income at your target retirement age.

Take Control Of Your Retirement Planning


The true advantage of Calculate My Pension lies in its interactive capabilities. You can adjust the input variables and instantly see how these changes affect your retirement plan. This real-time feedback provides a clear understanding of the best strategies to reach your financial goals.

Additionally, Calculate My Pension allows you to plan your retirement in conjunction with your spouse or partner, focusing on achieving a combined income that meets both of your needs in retirement.

Calculate My Pension puts you in the driving seat of your retirement journey by providing visibility of the outcomes and the risks, allowing you to make the adjustments necessary to secure a financially stable and enjoyable future.

Value of Your Funds at Retirement
Your pension fund and ISAs will be accumulated up to your chosen retirement age, factoring in your ongoing contributions and projected fund growth.
Sustainable Retirement Income
The maximum sustainable income you can withdraw without running out of funds will be calculated. This is done by simulating your retirement many times and adjusting your income up or down based on the funds remaining each time.
Fund Progression
The progression of your funds over time will be plotted in a chart, showing the accumulation of your funds up to retirement and then the drawdown phase as income is taken.
Retirement Income Breakdown
You will see a breakdown of your retirement income between the various sources in each retirement year, including your state pension, and allowing for the different sources to commence payment at different ages.
