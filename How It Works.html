The 3 Stages of the Calculation
Accumulate Funds To Retirement
The first step is to accumulate your fund with your planned regular contributions and fund growth up to your chosen retirement date.
Calculate Maximum Affordable Retirment Income
Payments from your funds in retirement are then simulated multiple times, adjusting the values each time in order to target a final fund of zero. The payment that hits this target is your maximum affordable retirement income. If this is less than your desired retirement income, the shortfall is calculated.
Optimise Drawdown Plan for Tax Efficiency
If you have ISA funds to draw from, the split of payments between pension and ISA is then calculated each year to provide a tax efficient drawdown plan.
